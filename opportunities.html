Join the SMILE Lab and gain hands-on research experience in interdisciplinary engineering.

Join the SMILE Lab

The Soni Microfluidics & Interfacial Engineering Laboratory (SMILE Lab) welcomes motivated undergraduate students who are interested in applying engineering principles to solve real-world scientific challenges. Whether you are exploring research for the first time or preparing for graduate school, our lab provides a collaborative environment where students gain hands-on experience while contributing to interdisciplinary research.

Current Opportunities

Undergraduate Research
Research Credit

The SMILE Lab is actively recruiting undergraduate students interested in conducting research for academic credit during the academic year.

Summer Research Positions

Applications are also welcomed from students interested in summer research opportunities. Summer positions are available based on funding and laboratory capacity.

Why Join the SMILE Lab?

🔬

Hands-on Research

Contribute to interdisciplinary research in microfluidics, electrohydrodynamics, environmental health, and advanced materials.

🤝

Faculty Mentorship

Work closely with Dr. Soni in a collaborative and supportive research environment.

🚀

Professional Development

Build technical, analytical, and communication skills while preparing for graduate school and engineering careers.

How to Apply

Interested students should email Dr. Soni with:

  • A brief statement describing your research interests
  • A résumé
